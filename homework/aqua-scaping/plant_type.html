Picking Your Plants

Picking the plants that you chose to put in your aqua-scape is the backbone of any setup. If you chose the wrong plants you can end up with a medley of disasters. So best to avoid them. First you want to chose whether you want easy maintenance plants, medium or difucult plants to take care of. Next you need to decide plants for the Front(foreground), Middle, and plants for the Back(Background) For this webpage everything is going to be for Beginners.
