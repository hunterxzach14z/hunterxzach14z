Weather Page Planning Doc.

Purpose Statement

The reason for making this website is to provide the weather for the average citizen/tourist. There is no target income or education. I would say that the target age would be that of 18+. This webpage is being created for those that want to know what the weather is going to be like so that they can plan accordingly whether it be how they dress or what they do for the day. In this they should know what the temperature is going to be, whether it is going to windy, rainy, or sunny, and whatever else seems to be relevant.

Visitor Analysis

The target audience is age 18-99+. Whether they be citizens or tourists. Income isn’t really a factor as it is relevant to everyone. This content will be organized by city. To make the mobile experience more enjoyable we are going to make sure that the info shown is able to scale down so that the user doesn’t have to scroll sideways to view data. This website would mainly be accessed in the morning, but could otherwise be accessed throughout the day.

Persona

Kim Smith Kim is 30 years old and a mother to 3 kids who are involved with sports. She is a middle class citizen with a full time job. She needs to be able to look at the weather in the morning before her kids are awake so that she can advise them on how to dress for the day. She needs this information to be easily accessible using her cell phone. The site needs to be able to load quickly because as a mother and a worker she need to be able to finish any work/ chores before leaving the house. “Rain Rain Go Away!”
